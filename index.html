Our Heritage

Roots that run deeper than the valleys.

Durga Madhab Coffee and Pepper Estate began its coffee journey in 1998 under the stewardship of Trilochan Satapathy. What started as a small initiative has grown into a flourishing plantation spread across 15 acres, producing nearly 4,500 kg of coffee annually.

The coffee cultivated here is known for its distinct taste and rich aroma, shaped by the unique soil and climate of the Koraput region. Over the years, the farm’s produce gained attention for qualities that set it apart from conventional plantation coffee.
