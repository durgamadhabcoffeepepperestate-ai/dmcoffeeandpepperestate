Our Heritage

Roots that run deeper than the valleys.

Durga Madhab Coffee and Pepper Estate began its coffee journey in 1998 under the stewardship of Trilochan Satapathy. What started as a small initiative has grown into a flourishing plantation spread across 15 acres, producing nearly 4,500 kg of coffee annually.

The coffee cultivated here is known for its distinct taste and rich aroma, shaped by the unique soil and climate of the Koraput region. Over the years, the farm’s produce gained attention for qualities that set it apart from conventional plantation coffee.

The Estate Process

01

1️⃣ Plantation & Natural Growth

Our journey begins in rich, fertile soil where coffee and pepper plants grow under natural shade. The estate follows traditional farming practices that respect the land, climate, and biodiversity.

02

2️⃣ Flowering Stage

Seasonal rains trigger beautiful white coffee blossoms and healthy pepper spikes. This stage defines the future quality and aroma of the produce.

03

3️⃣ Cherry & Pepper Development

Coffee cherries slowly ripen from green to deep red, while pepper berries mature on climbing vines. Careful monitoring ensures optimal ripeness.

04

4️⃣ Hand Harvesting

Only mature coffee cherries and healthy pepper spikes are handpicked. This selective harvesting preserves flavor integrity and ensures premium quality.

05

5️⃣ Sun Drying

Freshly harvested produce is naturally sun-dried on raised mats. This slow, traditional drying process enhances aroma and shelf stability without artificial heat.

06

6️⃣ Processing & Sorting

Coffee beans are hulled and graded, while peppercorns are cleaned and sorted. Defective or broken produce is removed to maintain consistency.

07

7️⃣ Final Green Beans & Peppercorns

The result is high-quality green coffee beans and bold, aromatic peppercorns—ready for roasting, trading, or further artisanal processing.

08

8️⃣ Farm to Legacy

Every batch carries the story of our land, our people, and generations of farming knowledge—crafted with patience, pride, and purity.
